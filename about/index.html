About us

Enhancing everyday experiences through software and technology

Kitchon strives to create unique experiences through constantly evolving innovation and technology. Leveraging state of the art AI and cutting edge development technologies, we strive to deliver intuitive and hands free experience's to our users around the world. Since our inception in 2018, Kitchon's small team of engineers worked collaborativly to create an app to empower amatuear and professional chefs to create stunning meals with step by step guidance.

Our team

At Kitchon, we believe inclusive and focused collaboration can enable even a small team to turn big ideas into reality. But we can't do it alone, we are looking for talented android and ios developers to help build out our first alpha release. If you have the skills, and are interested in supporting the future of AI enabled cooking, we'd love to have you on team! Reach out contact our core developers, we'd love to learn more about how your skills can help tranform the cooking experience.
